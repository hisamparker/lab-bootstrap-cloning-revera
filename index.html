stuffed triple chocolate

Chewy triple chocolate cookies featuring white, milk, and semisweet chocolate chunks! Crisp on the edge and extra chewy, these cookies are a chocolate lover’s dream. These triple chocolate cookies can be made start to finish in under an hour. They also use everyday baking staples, like all purpose flour, cocoa powder, and light brown sugar.
